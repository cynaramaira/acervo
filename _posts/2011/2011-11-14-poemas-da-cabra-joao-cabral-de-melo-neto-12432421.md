<p><p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Nas margens do Mediterr&acirc;neo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o se v&ecirc; um palmo de terra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a terra tivesse esquecido<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de fazer converter em pedra.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Nas margens do Mediterr&acirc;neo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; N&atilde;o se v&ecirc; um palmo de pedra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a pedra tivesse esquecido<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de ocupar com sua fera.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ali, onde nenhuma linha<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; pode lembrar, porque mais doce,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; o que at&eacute; chega a parecer<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; suave serra de uma foice,<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o se v&ecirc; um palmo de terra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; por mais pedra ou fera que seja,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a cabra n&atilde;o tenha ocupado<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; com sua planta fibrosa e negra.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 1<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra &eacute; negra. Mas seu negro<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o &eacute; o negro do &eacute;bano douto<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (que &eacute; quase azul) ou o negro rico<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; do jacarand&aacute; (mais bem roxo).<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O negro da cabra &eacute; o negro<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; do preto, do pobre, do pouco.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negro da poeira, que &eacute; cinzento.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negro da ferrugem, que &eacute; fosco.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negro do feio, &agrave;s vezes branco.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ou o negro do pardo, que &eacute; pardo.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; disso que n&atilde;o chega a ter cor<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ou perdeu toda cor no gasto.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute; o negro da segunda classe.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Do inferior (que &eacute; sempre opaco).<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Disso que n&atilde;o pode ter cor<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; porque em negro sai mais barato.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Se o negro quer dizer noturno<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; o negro da cabra &eacute; solar.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; N&atilde;o &eacute; o da cabra o negro noite.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute; o negro de sol. Luminar.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ser&aacute; o negro do queimado<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; mais que o negro da escurid&atilde;o.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negra &eacute; do sol que acumulou.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute; o negro mais bem do carv&atilde;o.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; N&atilde;o &eacute; o negro do macabro.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negro funeral. Nem do luto.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Tampouco &eacute; o negro do mist&eacute;rio,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de bra&ccedil;os cruzados, eunuco.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute; mesmo o negro do carv&atilde;o.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O negro da hulha. Do coque.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Negro que pode haver na p&oacute;lvora:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; negro de vida, n&atilde;o de morte.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 3<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O negro da cabra &eacute; o negro<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; da natureza dela cabra.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mesmo dessa que n&atilde;o &eacute; negra,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; como a do Moxot&oacute;, que &eacute; clara.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O negro &eacute; o duro que h&aacute; no fundo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; da cabra. De seu natural.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Tal no fundo da terra h&aacute; pedra,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; no fundo da pedra, metal.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O negro &eacute; o duro que h&aacute; no fundo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; da natureza sem orvalho<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que &eacute; a da cabra, esse animal<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sem folhas, s&oacute; raiz e talo,<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que &eacute; a da cabra, esse animal<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de alma-caro&ccedil;o, de alma c&oacute;rnea,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sem moelas, &uacute;midos, l&aacute;bios,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; p&atilde;o sem miolo, apenas c&ocirc;dea.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 4<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Quem j&aacute; encontrou uma cabra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que tivesse ritmos dom&eacute;sticos&#63;<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O grosso derrame do porco,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; da vaca, do sono e de t&eacute;dio&#63;<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Quem encontrou cabra que fosse<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; animal de sociedade&#63;<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Tal o c&atilde;o, o gato, o cavalo,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; diletos do homem e da arte&#63;<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra guarda todo o arisco,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; rebelde, do animal selvagem,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; viva demais que &eacute; para ser<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; animal dos de luxo ou pajem.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Viva demais para n&atilde;o ser,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; quando colaboracionista,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; o reduzido irredut&iacute;vel,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; o inconformado conformista.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 5<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra &eacute; o melhor instrumento<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de verrumar a terra magra.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Por dentro da serra e da seca<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o chega onde chega a cabra.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Se a serra &eacute; terra, a cabra &eacute; pedra.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Se a serra &eacute; pedra, &eacute; pedernal.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sua boca &eacute; sempre mais dura<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a serra, n&atilde;o importa qual.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra tem o dente frio,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a insol&ecirc;ncia do que mastiga.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Por isso o homem vive da cabra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; mas sempre a v&ecirc; como inimiga.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Por isso quem vive da cabra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; e n&atilde;o &eacute; capaz do seu bra&ccedil;o<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; desconfia sempre da cabra:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; diz que tem parte com o Diabo.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 6<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; N&atilde;o &eacute; pelo v&iacute;cio da pedra,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; por preferir a pedra &agrave; folha.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute; que a cabra &eacute; expulsa do verde,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; trancada do lado de fora.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra &eacute; trancada por dentro.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Condenada &agrave; caatinga seca.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Liberta, no vasto sem nada,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; proibida, na verdura estreita.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Leva no pesco&ccedil;o uma canga<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a impede de furar as cercas.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Leva os muros do pr&oacute;prio c&aacute;rcere:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; prisioneira e carcereira.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Liberdade de fome e sede<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; da ambulante prisioneira.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; N&atilde;o &eacute; que ela busque o dif&iacute;cil:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &eacute; que a sabem capaz de pedra.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 7<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A vida da cabra n&atilde;o deixa<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; lazer para ser fina ou l&iacute;rica<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (tal o urubu, que em doces linhas<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; voa &agrave; procura da carni&ccedil;a).<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Vive a cabra contra a pendente,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sem os &ecirc;xtases das decidas.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Viver para a cabra n&atilde;o &eacute;<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; re-ruminar-se introspectiva.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &Eacute;, literalmente, cavar<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a vida sob a superf&iacute;cie,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que a cabra, proibida de folhas,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; tem de desentranhar ra&iacute;zes.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Eis porque &eacute; a cabra grosseira,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; de m&atilde;os &aacute;speras, realista.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Eis porque, mesmo ruminando,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o &eacute; jamais contemplativa.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 8<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O n&uacute;cleo de cabra &eacute; vis&iacute;vel<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; por debaixo de muitas coisas.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Com a natureza da cabra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; outras aprendem sua crosta.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Um n&uacute;cleo de cabra &eacute; vis&iacute;vel<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; em certos atributos roucos<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que t&ecirc;m as coisas obrigadas<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a fazer de seu corpo couro.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A fazer de seu couro sola,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a armar-se em coura&ccedil;as, escamas:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; como se d&aacute; com certas coisas<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; e muitas condi&ccedil;&otilde;es humanas.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Os jumentos s&atilde;o animais<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que muito aprenderam com a cabra.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O nordestino, convivendo-a,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; fez-se de sua mesma casta.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 9<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O n&uacute;cleo de cabra &eacute; vis&iacute;vel<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; debaixo do homem do Nordeste.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Da cabra lhe vem o escarpado<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; e o estofo nervudo que o enche.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Se adivinha o n&uacute;cleo de cabra<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; no jeito de existir, Cardozo,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que reponta sob seu gesto<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; como esqueleto sob o corpo.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; E &eacute; outra ossatura mais forte<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; que o esqueleto comum, de todos;<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; debaixo do pr&oacute;prio esqueleto,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; no fundo centro de seus ossos.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A cabra deu ao nordestino<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; esse esqueleto mais de dentro:<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; o a&ccedil;o do osso, que resiste<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; quando o osso perde seu cimento.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; *<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; O Mediterr&acirc;neo &eacute; mar cl&aacute;ssico,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; com &aacute;guas de m&aacute;rmore azul.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Em nada me lembra das &aacute;guas<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sem marca do rio Paje&uacute;.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; As ondas do Mediterr&acirc;neo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; est&atilde;o no m&aacute;rmore tra&ccedil;adas.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Nos rios do Sert&atilde;o, se existe,<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a &aacute;gua corre despenteada.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; As margens do Mediterr&acirc;neo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; parecem deserto balc&atilde;o.<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Deserto, mas de terras nobres<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; n&atilde;o da pi&ccedil;arra do Sert&atilde;o.<br /></p>
<p><br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mas n&atilde;o minto o Mediterr&acirc;neo<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; nem sua atmosfera maior<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; descrevendo-lhe as cabras negras<br /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; em termos da do Moxot&oacute;.</p></p>
